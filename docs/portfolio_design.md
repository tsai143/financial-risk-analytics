\# Portfolio Design \& Business Understanding



\## Objective

The objective of this portfolio is to analyze investment risk, volatility,

asset correlation, and future portfolio behavior using quantitative methods.



\## Portfolio Construction Logic

The portfolio is designed to achieve sector diversification in order to:

\- Reduce concentration risk

\- Study correlation impact on portfolio volatility

\- Simulate realistic investment scenarios
